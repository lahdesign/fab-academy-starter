Lara Henderson

Book Artist, Printmaker, Educator

Providence, Rhode Island

About

Lara is a book artist, printmaker and educator based in Providence, RI. Lara attended the University of Massachusetts, Dartmouth for her BFA in Graphic Design/ Letterform. In 2010, she completed her MFA at the University of the Arts in Book Arts/ Printmaking, focusing on book structures and offset lithography. After completing her MFA, Lara was the Manager of the AS220 Printshop, a community public-access printmaking studio for three years. She now balances her time between teaching at Brown University and UMass Dartmouth, being the Book Arts Coordinator at AS220 and as a student of Fab Academy.
